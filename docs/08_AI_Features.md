# AI Features

## Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is a core component of the HR Platform.

Its purpose is to assist employees, managers, and HR teams by automating repetitive tasks, providing recommendations, and improving decision-making.

AI should always act as an assistant, not as a replacement for human decisions.

---

# AI-001 HR Chatbot

## Description

Provide an AI-powered chatbot capable of answering HR-related questions.

### Examples

- How many leave days do I have left?
- How do I request a leave?
- Where can I find my contract?
- What is the onboarding process?
- Explain the Employee Handbook.

---

# AI-002 Intelligent Document Search

The AI should help users quickly find documents using natural language.

Example:

> "Show me my latest payslip."

---

# AI-003 Document Summarization

The AI should summarize HR documents.

Examples:

- Employment contracts
- Company policies
- Employee Handbook
- HR procedures

---

# AI-004 Email Generation

The AI should assist HR by generating professional emails.

Examples:

- Welcome emails
- Interview invitations
- Leave responses
- Contract reminders

---

# AI-005 Onboarding Assistant

The AI should guide new employees through onboarding.

Examples:

- Explain each onboarding task
- Recommend the next step
- Answer onboarding questions

---

# AI-006 Manager Assistant

The AI should assist managers by:

- Summarizing employee performance
- Highlighting pending approvals
- Suggesting meeting agendas
- Identifying overdue tasks

---

# AI-007 HR Assistant

The AI should assist HR teams by:

- Summarizing employee profiles
- Classifying HR documents
- Detecting missing documents
- Suggesting onboarding tasks
- Preparing HR reports

---

# AI-008 Smart Notifications

The AI should prioritize notifications based on urgency.

Examples:

- Contract expiration
- Missing mandatory documents
- Upcoming performance reviews
- Pending leave approvals

---

# AI-009 Knowledge Assistant

The AI should answer questions about company policies using the Employee Handbook and HR documentation.
